BIG SKY PARASAIL - SAFETY AND REQUIREMENTS

=== SAFETY RECORD ===
Big Sky Parasail has completed over 25,000 safe flights across 20+ years of parasailing operations with a 100% safety record. Safety is our absolute top priority.

=== CERTIFICATIONS ===
- USCG Licensed Master Captain (100-Ton Near Coastal)
- USCG Inspected Passenger Vessel (the Cloud Dancer)
- Coast Guard certified parasailing equipment
- Regular safety inspections and equipment maintenance
- All crew members undergo regular safety training

=== AGE REQUIREMENTS ===
- Minimum age: 6 years old
- Children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult on the flight (tandem)
- No maximum age — parasailing is gentle and suitable for most fitness levels

=== WEIGHT REQUIREMENTS ===
- Single flyers: 80-350 lbs
- Tandem flights (doubles): combined weight limits apply
- Triple flights: combined weight limits apply
- Contact us at (406) 270-6256 if you have specific weight questions

=== SWIMMING ABILITY ===
Swimming ability is NOT required. All parasailers wear Coast Guard approved life jackets throughout the entire experience. Our crew is trained in water safety and rescue procedures. Most flights involve dry takeoffs and landings directly from the boat — you may not even touch the water.

=== WHAT TO WEAR ===
- Comfortable clothes you don't mind getting wet (athletic wear, shorts, t-shirt)
- Secure footwear: water shoes or sandals with straps (no flip-flops)
- Sunscreen and sunglasses with a strap
- Optional: hat, light layer (Montana lake water can be cool)
- Leave valuables in your car or with observers on the boat
- We provide life jackets

=== WEATHER POLICY ===
Safety comes first. If weather conditions are unsafe for parasailing (high winds, storms, lightning, poor visibility), we will:
- Contact you in advance if conditions look unfavorable
- Reschedule your flight at no charge
- Never fly in unsafe conditions

You can also reschedule for any reason with advance notice.

=== CANCELLATION POLICY ===
- Weather-related cancellations: always rescheduled at no charge
- Customer cancellations: please contact us as soon as possible
- We offer flexible rescheduling
- Contact us at (406) 270-6256 or bigskyparasailing@gmail.com

=== SAFETY BRIEFING ===
Every flight includes a thorough safety briefing covering:
- Equipment overview and harness fitting
- Takeoff and landing procedures
- Hand signals and communication
- What to expect during the flight
- Emergency procedures

=== FLIGHT TYPES ===
- Single: one person flies alone
- Tandem (doubles): two people fly together on the same chute
- Triple: three people fly together on the same chute
- Great for families and groups who want to share the experience

=== THE BOAT ===
The Cloud Dancer (Ocean Pro 31) is purpose-built for parasailing with:
- Professional-grade hydraulic winch for smooth takeoffs and landings
- Spacious deck for passengers
- Coast Guard inspected and certified
- Capacity: up to 10 passengers per trip
- Equipped with all required safety gear

=== MEDICAL CONSIDERATIONS ===
If you have any of the following, please consult your doctor before booking:
- Heart conditions
- Back or neck problems
- Pregnancy
- Recent surgery
- Severe motion sickness
Contact us if you have questions about whether parasailing is right for you.
