=== PARKING ===
There is plenty of FREE parking available at Flathead Harbor Marina. The marina entrance is well-marked on Highway 93 — look for the Flathead Harbor sign. We recommend arriving 15 minutes before your scheduled departure time to park and check in.

=== DRIVING DIRECTIONS ===

From Kalispell (15 miles):
Head south on US-93 S for approximately 15 miles. Flathead Harbor will be on your left in Lakeside.

From Whitefish (25 miles):
Head south on US-93 S through Kalispell, continue south for about 35 minutes total.

From Glacier National Park (40 miles):
Head southwest on US-2 W, then south on US-93 S. The marina is on the left in Lakeside.

From Missoula (90 miles):
Head north on US-93 N for approximately 2 hours. Flathead Harbor will be on your right as you enter Lakeside.

=== FLYING IN ===
Glacier Park International Airport (FCA) is just 30 miles away in Kalispell, with service from major airlines. It's the closest major airport to our marina.

=== KEY DISTANCES ===

- Whitefish: 25 miles (resort town)
- Glacier Park International Airport (FCA): 30 miles

=== ABOUT FLATHEAD HARBOR MARINA ===
Flathead Harbor Marina is a full-service facility on the western shore of Flathead Lake.

Marina Services:
- Seasonal slip rentals
- Boat rentals
- Fuel dock
- Boat tours

Marina Amenities:
- Harbor Grille restaurant (on-site dining)
- Anchor Bar (on-site bar)
- Convenience store
- Public restrooms

Marina Website: https://www.flatheadharbor.com/

=== WHY WE CHOSE THIS LOCATION ===
We chose Flathead Harbor Marina because it offers:
- Easy lake access for safe takeoffs and landings
- Stunning mountain views in every direction
- Great amenities for our customers (food, drinks, restrooms)
- Central location on the west shore of Flathead Lake

=== NEARBY ATTRACTIONS ===

Glacier National Park (40 miles north):
Crown jewel of the National Park System with majestic mountains, pristine forests, and alpine meadows.

Wild Horse Island (short boat ride from marina):
The largest island on Flathead Lake, home to wild horses, bighorn sheep, and pristine hiking trails. We offer a Wild Horse Island boat charter for $2,000 (4 hours, up to 12 guests).

Bigfork (20 minutes by car):
Charming village known for its art galleries, fine dining, and summer theater.

Whitefish (35 minutes by car):
Year-round activities including hiking, mountain biking, and scenic lift rides at Whitefish Mountain Resort.

Kalispell (15 miles):
Shopping, dining, and the gateway to Glacier National Park.

=== WEATHER ===
Check current weather conditions for Flathead Lake at weather.com. Montana summers (May-September) typically feature warm days (70-90°F), cool mornings, and long daylight hours. Lake temperatures range from the mid-50s to low 70s°F during summer. Afternoon winds can pick up, which is why our Early Bird morning flights offer the calmest conditions.
