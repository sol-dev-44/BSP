ABOUT US:
Big Sky Parasail is Montana's premier parasailing company, operating on Flathead Lake — the largest natural freshwater lake west of the Mississippi River. With over 20 years of parasailing experience and more than 25,000 safe flights completed, we are the most trusted parasailing operation in the region. We maintain a perfect 5-star rating and a 100% safety record.

Our team holds USCG (United States Coast Guard) licensing and certifications. Our captain is a USCG Licensed Master Captain (100-Ton Near Coastal) with extensive parasailing experience. The vessel is a USCG Inspected Passenger Vessel, meeting the highest safety standards for commercial parasailing operations.

SISTER COMPANY:
Big Sky Parasail is affiliated with Havasu Parasail, which operates on Lake Havasu in Arizona. Visit them at havasuparasail.com.

SEASON:
We operate from May 1st through September 30th, daily from 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM (weather permitting). Our schedule adjusts slightly based on Montana's solar calendar — September has an earlier last flight (6:00 PM) due to earlier sunsets.

THE EXPERIENCE:
Guests soar up to 400+ feet above the crystal-clear waters of Flathead Lake. From this altitude, you'll see panoramic views of the Mission Mountains, Glacier National Park in the distance, the Flathead Valley, and the stunning blue-green waters of the lake. Total trip time is approximately 1-1.5 hours, including check-in, safety briefing, boat ride, and flight. Actual flight time in the air is typically 10-15 minutes.

OUR VESSEL - THE CLOUD DANCER:
We fly from the Cloud Dancer, an Ocean Pro 31 parasail boat. She holds up to 10 passengers per trip and is purpose-built for parasailing with a professional-grade hydraulic winch system for smooth flights and a spacious deck. The Cloud Dancer is Coast Guard inspected and certified for commercial parasailing operations.
