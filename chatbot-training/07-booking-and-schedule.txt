BIG SKY PARASAIL - BOOKING AND SCHEDULE DETAILS

=== HOW TO BOOK ===
1. Online (recommended): Visit montanaparasail.com/book
   - Instant confirmation
   - Real-time availability
   - Secure payment via Stripe
   - Select your preferred date, time, party size, and add-ons

2. Phone: Call (406) 270-6256

3. Email: bigskyparasailing@gmail.com

=== OPERATING SEASON ===
May 1st through September 30th
Daily operation, 7 days a week (weather permitting)

=== DAILY SCHEDULE ===
Hourly flights from 9:00 AM through the last flight of the day.

The last flight time varies by month based on Montana's solar schedule:
- May: Last flight at 7:00 PM (sunset ~8:45 PM)
- June: Last flight at 7:00 PM (sunset ~9:30 PM)
- July: Last flight at 7:00 PM (sunset ~9:20 PM)
- August: Last flight at 7:00 PM (sunset ~8:30 PM)
- September: Last flight at 6:00 PM (sunset ~7:30 PM)
